What Are Invisible Braces?

Invisible braces are orthodontic solutions designed to straighten teeth without the visibility of traditional metal braces. They may include clear aligners (like Invisalign), ceramic braces, or other discreet systems. The goal is to move teeth into proper alignment while minimizing appearance, irritation, and lifestyle disruption.
